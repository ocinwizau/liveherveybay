Eli Creek, K'gari

Float down the clearest freshwater creek on K'gari (Fraser Island), flowing directly onto 75 Mile Beach. One of the island's most iconic natural experiences — crystal clear, cold, and unforgettable.

📍 K'gari Island (via day tour) ⏱ Via day tour from Hervey Bay

Beaches

Urangan Pier & Beach & Beach

Walk one of Queensland's longest timber piers — 868 metres into the bay. A favourite for fishing, sunset watching, and dolphin spotting. Schools of fish and rays are visible in the clear water below.

📍 Pulgul St, Urangan QLD 4655 💰 Free 🎣 Fishing allowed

Reefworld Aquarium

One of the world's few all-natural aquariums, using only sand-filtered seawater and natural sunlight. Home to sharks, rays, turtles, and tropical fish. Operating since 1979 right on the Urangan foreshore.

📍 Cnr Kent & Pulgul St, Urangan QLD 4655 📞 (07) 4128 9828
Featured ★★★★☆ 4.5
